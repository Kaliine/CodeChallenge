What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small piece of information left on a visitor's computer by a website, via a web browser.
Cookies are used to personalize a user’s web experience with a website. It may contain the user’s preferences
or inputs when accessing that website. A user can customize their web browser to accept,
reject, or delete cookies.
